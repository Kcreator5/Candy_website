• Why will people come to your site?

If this was a legitimate site then people would probably come for the innovations in business, such as making/customizing your own candies. This is the case because there is no other business out there, that I know of, that allows it’s customers to make their own candies. The closest thing to this is websites that allow its customers to plaster a logo on pre-made candies.

• What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

What candies can I make? What flavors are available? Do they have chocolates? What type of sweets do they have? Can I submit my own design? Can I buy this in bulk or singles? Can I design my own and sell it to other people?

• What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site? Browsing selection, creating concepts, and buying candies. Shipping, • What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers? Prices, ingredients, calories, allergies, quantity, flavors, Size, shelf-life, deals, designs, and how to storage the food. • Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?
